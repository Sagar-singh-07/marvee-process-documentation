# User Roles and Access Process

This document explains how different users work in the Marvee
digitization system and how tasks move from one user to another.


## User Roles

### Supervisor
 Manages the complete workflow.
 Assigns work to all other users.
 Monitors progress and handles exceptions.
 Has full access to the system.

### DU Operator
 Uploads and processes documents assigned by the Supervisor.
 Updates the status after completing the work.
 Can access only assigned documents.

### QC2 Operator
 Checks the quality of processed documents.
 Approves correct work or sends it back for correction.
 Works only on tasks assigned by the Supervisor.

### Audit Operator
 Performs final verification of approved documents.
 Ensures data accuracy and compliance.
 Updates audit status after review.

## How the Work Is Done

1. The Supervisor logs in and checks pending documents.
2. The Supervisor assigns documents to the DU Operator.
3. The DU Operator uploads and processes the documents.
4. The Supervisor assigns completed work to the QC2 Operator.
5. The QC2 Operator checks quality and approves or sends back for correction.
6. Approved documents are assigned to the Audit Operator.
7. The Audit Operator verifies the documents and updates the status.
8. The Supervisor reviews the final output and closes the task.

## Summary

The system follows a simple role-based process.  
The Supervisor assigns work, operators complete their tasks step by step,
and the workflow moves forward only after approval at each stage.
